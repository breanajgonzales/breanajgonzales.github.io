About Me

Breana Gonzales

Full-Stack Developer

You would not guess it by my blonde hair and blue eyes, but I am a proud Mexican...and American...dual citizen really.

Born and raised in Chihuahua, Mexico, I love interacting with diverse groups of people and bridging differences in the workplace.

I am a results-driven, problem-solving, full-stack developer. I am thorough in my work and if the devil is in the details, I am pretty stiff competition. Delivering clean, bug-free code is my goal on every project. My strengths are clearly understanding business needs, communicating frequently and delivering what the customer wants on time - especially killer UIs.

When I'm not busy coding or being a mom, I also love composing songs on my guitar, hiking, traveling, half marathon training and learning all I can about new technologies and alternative medicine. My passions are beautiful and intuitive UIs, my family, diversity, and promoting web literacy.
